Privacy Policy & Data Agreement

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sahayak (The Protector). We are fully committed to protecting your personal details, credentials, and sensitive health information in compliance with medical data privacy guidelines.

2. Information We Collect

To provide tele-health counseling, symptom triage, and medical assistant features, we collect the following datasets:

  • Authentication Details: Username, email address, password hashes (encrypted via `bcrypt` on registration), and role designations (Student, Counsellor, Admin).
  • Mental & Physical Health Metrics: Answers submitted to the 9-point Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ-9), screening scores, wellness trends, and active medicine names/schedules added via MedAids.
  • Tele-Medicine & Booking Data: Appointment logs containing selected doctor (e.g. Dr. Sharma, Dr. Anjali), appointment date/time, selected consultation mode (video, audio, chat), and user-provided counseling reasons.
  • Real-time Location: Geolocation coordinates captured only upon user initiation to identify nearby medical centers or map nearest clinic markers.
  • AI Chatbot Conversations: Anonymized text conversations processed by our local Python AI chatbot backend for providing first aid or triage routing.

3. How We Use and Share Data

  • OTP Verification: Sending verification codes via Nodemailer and Twilio Service.
  • Doctor Consultations: Permitting counselors to review wellness history and scheduled appointment entries.
  • Emergency Response: Instantly dispatching location coordinates to emergency contacts or nearest ambulances upon triggering the SOS beacon.
  • Strict Confidentiality: Your mental wellness history, chat records, and symptoms are never sold or shared with third-party advertisers.

4. Data Protection & Security Measures

All network communications are processed securely. Authorization tokens are signed via JsonWebToken (JWT) on login, and medical screening scores are kept encrypted in storage. Users can delete their locally saved data by selecting the logout option.

Essentials of Mental Wellness

1. Sleep Hygiene

Restores brain function and emotional regulation.

  • The 10-3-2-1 Rule: No caffeine 10h before bed, no food 3h before, no work 2h before, no screens 1h before.
  • Temperature: Keep your room cool (approx 18-20°C) for deeper sleep.
  • Consistency: Waking up at the same time is more important than falling asleep at the same time.

2. Gut-Brain Connection

What you eat directly affects how you feel.

  • Hydration: Dehydration causes fatigue and brain fog. Drink 2-3L water daily.
  • Avoid the Crash: Sugary snacks give a burst of energy followed by anxiety/irritability. Choose nuts or fruit.
  • Omega-3s: Walnuts, flaxseeds, and fish help reduce inflammation in the brain.

3. Move to Improve

Motion creates emotion.

  • The 20-Min Rule: 20 minutes of moderate walking releases endorphins (natural painkillers).
  • Posture Check: Slouching signals "low power" to your brain. Sitting up straight can boost confidence.
  • Sunlight: Get morning sunlight to reset your biological clock and boost mood (Vitamin D).

4. Instant Grounding

Techniques to stop a panic spiral.

  • 5-4-3-2-1 Technique: Acknowledge 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.
  • Box Breathing: Inhale 4s, Hold 4s, Exhale 4s, Hold 4s. Repeats resets the nervous system.
  • Cold Water: Splash cold water on your face to trigger the "Mammalian Dive Reflex" which slows heart rate.

5. Digital Detox

  • Doomscrolling: Limit social media usage. It increases "Social Comparison" anxiety.
  • Grayscale Mode: Turn your phone screen to black & white to make it less addictive.
